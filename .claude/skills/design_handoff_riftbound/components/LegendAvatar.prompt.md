Circular champion/Legend avatar. The 2px ring is colored by the Legend's Runeterra `domain`; missing art falls back to the initial. `favorite` adds the gold star marker.

```jsx
<LegendAvatar legend={{ name: 'Ahri', domain: 'Calm', image_url }} size={52} favorite showName />
```

Use small sizes (28–36) in lists/pickers and matchup rows; large (64–80) in headers/profile.
