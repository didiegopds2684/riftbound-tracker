Tiny uppercase status/category pill. Tones map to brand colors; `favorite` adds the gold star marker used for a player's favorite Legend.

```jsx
<Badge label="Mais jogada" tone="gold" />
<Badge label="Favorito" favorite tone="gold" />
<Badge label="Nexus Nights" tone="neutral" outline />
```

Tones: `gold | cyan | win | loss | neutral`. `outline` for a hairline variant. For match results specifically, prefer `ResultBadge`.
